Story
After everything that's happened, this song offers hope for the beaten traveler off the beaten path. By believing in spirit, recovery, healing and forgiveness, a better day may still be possible. Before, the main dream was seen to be largely outside of one's self. But now this ongoing quest for the future is *also* married to an awareness of the present. And with this new appreciation of "now", the protagonist can begin dreaming anew...

Audio: 
 
Story
This song is a counterpart to "Over the Mountain". In 'dub-like' fashion, it evokes another dreamy better place. It's also a playful re-statement of purpose, first seen in "Over the Mountain". Basically the song offers hope that everything is and will be alright. And while again you're without words, this time it's not because you don't know what to say. Rather, it's that you're enjoying the moment and taking a well-deserved break.
