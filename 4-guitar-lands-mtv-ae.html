4 Guitar Lands on MTV and A&E

A portion of the song "4 Guitar" was used recently on MTV's "Catfish The TV Show". About 20 seconds is heard during "joe & kari ann", otherwise known as Season 1 Episode 7. Originally aired on 6/16/2013, it can be streamed for free at http://www.mtv.com/shows/catfish/catfish-the-tv-show-ep-7-joe-amp-kari-ann/1699627/playlist/.

About 10 seconds of this song was also used in Season 2, Episode 3 of A&E Network's "Monster In-Laws". The episode, titled "Beautii Is a Beast", originally aired June 4, 2012. There is no free streaming now, but it can be viewed at http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B0088WGI2G/.
