'4 Guitar' Appears on 365 Music Project

With absolutely no promotion, "4 Guitar" was featured on the Minneapolis website 365musicproject.org. Brandy blogs about local culture and highlights a *lot* of great music, while adding personal stories about anything and everything!  It's a fascinating blog, and we're psyched to have been found there.

New Best-of EP "Cliff Notes" Released

On July 4, in the spirit of freedom everywhere, James Land released "Cliff Notes" as a free download! This unexpected new EP is like a "best of" compilation, featuring 6 songs from last year's "After the Fact"...

We think this EP sums up the full album very well, and with fewer calories!  Anyway, it feels like summer and is available right here: http://store.jamesland.net/album/cliff-notes

Local Radio playing James Land

James Land's CD "After the Fact" has been added to the libraries of 2 college radio stations around Central Minnesota & the Twin Cities.  They are:

 

Both stations are playing selected tracks during their local music shows:

On KVSC-FM:  "The Area" with host John Drake from 7 - 10 p.m. every Monday
On WRFW-FM: "Local Bent" with host Elliot Novak each Monday from 3 - 5 p.m.

We'd like to thank these stations for supporting our local music!

"After the Fact" Review by Hello Music

August 3, 2010

Overall Rating: 3.33 (out of 5)

"An overall rating of 3.33 stars means this release is above average and stands out compared to most of the music we hear. A few areas could be stronger, but this is definitely a good release and you should be proud. You’re on the cusp of greatness and we think that with some hard work, you’ll get even better."

"We rarely tag a release as unique, so you should be proud. We hear stuff going on here that sounds fresh and isn't common. You clearly have a vision and you've started to realize it on your recordings. Keep up the good work."
